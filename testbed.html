List projects

EMBER Enabling Mental Health by Emotion Regulation

A smartphone app that forecasts emotion dysregulation episodes and offers a calming intervention.

TALOS Testbeds for Adaptive Learning and Observation of Social Systems

Foundational model of human decision-making trained in Minecraft worlds by collecting human gamer telemetry.

DYNAMOW Dynamic and AI Multimodal Modeling with Wearables

Foundational modeling in large-scale personal sensor data to stratify health risks.

STRIDES Suicide Triage and Risk Stratification with Integrated Data for EHR Systems

Decision-support tools that automatically stratify individual risk for suicide attempts using large-scale EHR data.

ABCD Projects Understanding how mental illness develops in youth

Largest long-term study of human development. Youth cohort followed from 8-20 years with ~80,000 repeated measures including Brain MRI.
